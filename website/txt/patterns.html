Coil Correction to Ensure Shell Continuity

✦ As a result of solving the integer Diophantine equation, we obtain the variants of geodesic coil correction that ensure the shell continuity.
✦ The geodesic coil is uniformly transformed into a non-geodesic coil with different degrees of deviation from the steady state.
✦ It is recommended to choose the variant with minimum deviation in percentage.
✦ The key parameter is the heading, which determines the coil pattern and to some extent the degree of interlacing of the layers.
