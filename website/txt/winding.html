Winding

  • Select the desired Safety ℝ which is the distance by which the center of the roller is offset from the mandrel surface.
  • Select the desired G-code length of the NC program in frames.
  • Calculate the coordinate motion by clicking the Winding button.
  • Control the animation using the Video player controls.
  • Control the appearance using the display Controls at the bottom of the screen.
  • Obtain your CNC program by clicking Download CNC button.
