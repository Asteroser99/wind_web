Visual Simulation of Machine Operation and CNC Program Generation

You can see in detail how the winding machine executes the calculated program of controlled coordinate motion.

For animate Winding process:

  • You need to select the desired Safety ‚Ñù which is the distance by which the center of the roller is offset from the mandrel surface.
  • You need to select the desired G-code length of the NC program in frames.
  • To calculate the coordinate motion you must click the Winding button.

Visualisation:

  • To control the animation you can use the Video player controls.
  • To control the the opacity of elements you can use the display Controls at the bottom of the screen:
  • - Mandrel
  • - Lines
  • - Tape
  • - Equidistanta

For downloading CNC program:

  • You can get your CNC program by clicking Download CNC button.
