Precise Calculation of Reinforcement Lines

The calculation of reinforcement lines starts with the calculation of the geodesic coil on the surface of rotation.
There are two initial parameters for this calculation - the radius of the pole hole and the width of the tape.
Having set these parameters to calculate the geodesic coil, click the button - Generate Coil.
You can see the result of the calculation on the mandrel surface.
The view of the result depends on the selected mode of displaying the keystrokes at the bottom of the screen.
The following modes of coil displaying are possible - line or tape.
