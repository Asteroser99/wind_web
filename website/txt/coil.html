Precise Calculation of Reinforcement Lines

The calculation of reinforcement lines starts with the calculation of the geodesic coil on the surface of rotation.
There are two initial parameters for this calculation - the radius of the pole hole and the width of the tape.
Having set these parameters to calculate the geodesic coil, click the button - Generate Coil.
You can see the result of the calculation on the mandrel surface.
The view of the result depends on the selected mode of displaying the keystrokes at the bottom of the screen.
The following modes of coil displaying are possible - line or tape.

Generating Coil

  • To calulate initial coil you must to select the initial value for the Pole radius ‚Ñù (up to the edge of tape).
  • Then you must set a tape width in input box Tape width.
  • To generate a geodesic coil you need to press the Generate Coil button.

Results:

  • You will see the Coilon the surface of the mandrel in the form depending on pressing one of the three buttons:

  • - First coil - only the first coil is shown;
  • - First round - N+1 turns are shown, where N is the Convenience;
  • - Full winding - shows the complete covering of the sheath with the tape.

  • Semi-transparency or visibility of elements is controlled by the corresponding buttons at the bottom of the screen in the right part.
  • The name of the button corresponds to the controlled element.
